Questions, answered

Straightforward answers for first-time visitors.

What is Available Margin?

Available Margin is the lowest projected checking balance over the next 90 days. It is an estimate based on your linked activity and detected patterns — not a guarantee.

Is this a budgeting app?

No. SimplBudget does not ask you to assign every purchase to a category. It focuses on whether your checking account stays okay as bills and income land.

How do bank connections work?

Connections use Plaid. You authenticate with your institution through Plaid’s secure flow. SimplBudget does not see or store your bank username or password.

When will it be available?

SimplBudget is coming soon on iPhone. Join the waitlist and we’ll email you when access opens.

Is this financial advice?

No. SimplBudget provides informational estimates only. Always review your own finances before making spending decisions.
